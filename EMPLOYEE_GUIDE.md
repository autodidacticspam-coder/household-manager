## Getting Started

### Installing TestFlight (iOS)

TestFlight is Apple's official app for testing apps before they're released on the App Store. You'll need it to install Household Manager on your iPhone or iPad.

**Step 1: Download TestFlight**
1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad
2. Tap the **Search** tab at the bottom
3. Type **"TestFlight"** in the search bar
4. Look for the app with the **blue airplane icon** by Apple
5. Tap **Get** (or the cloud icon if you've downloaded it before)
6. Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password
7. Wait for the download to complete

**Step 2: Open TestFlight**
1. Once installed, tap **Open** or find the TestFlight app on your home screen
2. The first time you open it, you may see a welcome screen - tap **Continue**

### Installing the App via TestFlight

Once you have TestFlight installed, you'll receive an invitation to test Household Manager.

**Method 1: Email Invitation**
1. Check your email for an invitation from TestFlight/Apple
2. Open the email on your iPhone or iPad
3. Tap the **"View in TestFlight"** or **"Start Testing"** button
4. TestFlight will open automatically
5. Tap **Accept** to join the beta
6. Tap **Install** next to Household Manager
7. Wait for the app to download and install
8. Tap **Open** to launch the app

**Method 2: Invitation Link**
1. Your manager will send you a TestFlight link (starts with `https://testflight.apple.com/...`)
2. Tap the link on your iPhone or iPad
3. If prompted, tap **Open in TestFlight**
4. Tap **Accept** to join the beta
5. Tap **Install** to download the app
6. Once installed, tap **Open**

**Method 3: Redemption Code**
1. Open TestFlight
2. Tap **Redeem** in the top-right corner
3. Enter the code provided by your manager
4. Tap **Redeem**
5. Tap **Install** next to Household Manager

### Using the Web Version

If you prefer to use the app on a computer or Android device, you can access it through a web browser:

1. Open any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
2. Go to: **https://household-manager-two.vercel.app**
3. Log in with your credentials (see next section)

**Tip:** On your phone, you can add the website to your home screen for quick access:
- **iPhone/Safari:** Tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
- **Android/Chrome:** Tap the menu (3 dots) → "Add to Home Screen"

## Logging In

### First-Time Login

1. Open the Household Manager app or website
2. You'll see the login screen
3. Enter your **email address** that was registered by your manager
4. Tap/click **Continue**
5. Check your email for a login link (passwordless login)
6. Tap the link in your email to complete login
7. You'll be automatically signed in and redirected to the app

### Returning Users

- The app will remember your login for 7 days
- If you're logged out, simply repeat the login process above
- For quick access, use the same device and browser

## My Tasks

This is where you'll spend most of your time - viewing and completing tasks assigned to you.

### Viewing Tasks

1. Tap **My Tasks** in the navigation
2. You'll see two tabs at the top:
   - **Assigned** - Tasks you need to complete
   - **Completed** - Finished tasks

3. Each task card shows:
   - Task title
   - Category (color-coded): Childcare, Cleaning, Cooking, Laundry, etc.
   - Priority level: Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
   - Due date/time
   - Description (tap to expand)

### Completing a Task

1. Find the task in the **Assigned** tab
2. Tap the **Complete** button (checkmark icon)
3. The task moves to the **Completed** tab

### Viewing Task Details

1. Tap on any task card to see full details
2. You may see:
   - Full description
   - Video instructions (if attached)
   - Start and end times (for timed tasks)
   - Notes from your manager

### Undoing a Completed Task

Made a mistake? You can undo a completed task:
1. Go to the **Completed** tab
2. Find the task
3. Tap **Undo** to move it back to Assigned

1. Tap **My Calendar** in the navigation

### Tapping on Events

- Tap any event to see details

## Time-Off Requests

Request vacation days, sick leave, or other time off.

### Submitting a New Request

1. Tap **Time-Off** in the navigation
2. Tap the **+ New Request** button
3. Fill out the form:
   - **Type:** Select Vacation or Sick Leave
   - **Select Days Off:** Tap on the calendar to select the days you want off
     - Tap a date to select it (tap again to deselect)
     - You can select multiple non-consecutive days
     - Selected days appear as badges above the calendar
     - Use "Clear all" to start over
   - **Full Day or Partial Day:** (only available when selecting a single day)
     - Toggle "Full Day" off to request partial time
     - Select start and end times for partial day requests
   - **Reason:** Brief explanation (optional but helpful)
4. Tap **Submit**
5. A confirmation dialog will appear reminding you that the system does not track remaining leave balances
6. Tap **I Understand, Submit Request** to confirm

### Checking Request Status

Your requests will show one of these statuses:
- **Pending** (yellow) - Waiting for manager approval
- **Approved** (green) - Your time off is confirmed
- **Denied** (red) - Request was not approved (check admin notes for reason)

### Canceling a Request

You can only cancel **pending** requests:
1. Find the pending request
2. Tap **Cancel Request**
3. Confirm the cancellation

### Viewing Admin Notes

If your manager added notes to your request:
1. Tap on the request to expand it
2. Look for "Admin Notes" section
3. This may explain approval conditions or denial reasons

## Supply Requests

Need supplies for your work? Request them here.

### Submitting a Supply Request

1. Tap **Supplies** in the navigation
2. Tap **+ New Request**
3. Fill out the form:
   - **Title:** What you need (e.g., "Cleaning supplies")
   - **Description:** Details about the items
   - **Product Link:** (Optional) Paste a URL to the exact product
4. Tap **Submit**

### Request Statuses

- **Pending** - Waiting for approval
- **Approved** - Your request was approved (supplies will be ordered)
- **Rejected** - Request was denied (check admin notes)

### Canceling a Supply Request

1. Find your pending request
2. Tap **Cancel**
3. Confirm cancellation

## Tips & Troubleshooting

### General Tips

1. **Check tasks daily** - Stay on top of new assignments
2. **Complete tasks promptly** - Mark them done as soon as you finish
3. **Request time off early** - Give your manager time to plan coverage
4. **Keep your profile updated** - Especially emergency contact info

### App Not Working?

**Try these steps:**

1. **Force close and reopen the app**
   - iPhone: Swipe up from bottom and swipe the app away
   - Reopen the app

2. **Check your internet connection**
   - Make sure you have WiFi or cellular data
   - Try loading a website to test

3. **Update the app**
   - Open TestFlight
   - Check if there's an update available
   - Tap **Update** if available

4. **Clear login and try again**
   - Log out and log back in
   - Check your email for the login link

### Can't Log In?

1. **Check your email address** - Make sure it matches what your manager registered
2. **Check spam/junk folder** - Login emails sometimes go there
3. **Wait a few minutes** - Sometimes emails are delayed
4. **Contact your manager** - They can verify your email is correct

### Tasks Not Showing?

1. **Pull down to refresh** - Swipe down on the task list
2. **Check all tabs** - The task might be in a different status
3. **Check your internet** - Tasks need internet to load

### TestFlight Issues

**App expired?**
- TestFlight apps expire after 90 days
- Wait for your manager to send a new build
- You'll get a notification when it's available

**Can't find the app?**
- Open TestFlight and look for Household Manager
- If it's not there, ask for a new invitation link

### Need Help?

Contact your manager if you experience:
- Login problems
- Missing tasks or incorrect assignments
- App crashes or errors
- Any other issues
